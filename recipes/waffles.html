Waffle Recipe

Description

Are you more pancake or waffle? Because I LOVE waffles. I like pancakes too, but waffles are totally my jam. The way that the syrup gets trapped in the little wells of the waffle... unmatched deliciousness.

These waffles are Belgian-inspired, so they're rectangular instead of circular. I recommend heating these in the toaster, toaster-oven, or even in the oven if you have the patience to preheat it! If you don't mind losing some crunch, go for the good old microwave. When heated, they smell like a legitimate vanilla candle, and they taste phenomenal. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 4 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup white granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs, separated
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Step 1: Combine flour, sugar and baking powder in a bowl.
  2. Step 2: In another bowl, mix together oil, egg yolks, vanilla extract and milk.
  3. Step 3: In a clean metal bowl, whip egg whites with electric mixed until stiff peaks.
  4. Step 4: Combine wet and dry ingredients until incorporated.
  5. Step 5: Fold in egg whites.
  6. Step 6: Pour batter into waffle iron. Yields about 10 waffles (5 2-piece rectangular waffles).
