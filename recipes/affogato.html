Affogato Recipe

Alright. I'll come clean. This isn't even really a recipe. I just needed to call attention to this fabulous combination. This Italian dessert is perfect for those who love coffee-centered treats. I'm not super into fancy coffees or blended drinks. I love my coffee black, but even this is such a good little (lazy) dessert for even the blackest of coffee-lovers.

Enjoy this super easy not-recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1-2 (or 3...) scoops of quality vanilla ice cream
  • 1 short high-quality espresso (I recommend Borbone, but any will do!)

Steps

  1. Step 1: Brew a fresh espresso. It's important it is hot!
  2. Step 2: Put vanilla ice cream in a bowl or glass cup.
  3. Step 3: Pour hot espresso over ice cream.
  4. Step 4: Enjoy!
