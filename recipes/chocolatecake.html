Chocolate Cake Recipe

Description

I'm going to be honest. I usually hate chocolate. Well, that's an exaggeration - I dislike a lot of chocolate-flavoured things. Chocolate ice cream, chocolate cookies, chocolate waffles... Even most chocolate cakes! I never find that the chocolate flavour is forward enough.

Because of my distaste for chocolate-flavoured treats, I really wanted to make a chocolate cake that was really chocolate-y. Kind of like a big brownie (with frosting!) I hope you enjoy this chocolate cake recipe as much as my family and I do.

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups all purpose-flour
  • 3/4 Dutch-processed cocoa powder
  • 2 cups white granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 cup boiling water or hot coffee

Steps

  1. Step 1: Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Step 2: Grease a bundt pan with vegetable oil.
  3. Step 3: Combine flour, cocoa powder and salt in a bowl.
  4. Step 4: In a separate bowl, mix together the eggs, oil and sugar until it is a homogenized mixture.
  5. Step 5: Add dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, add in the milk, and mix thoroughly.
  6. Step 6: Stir in boiling water. Be careful to not splash hot water as you mix.
  7. Step 7: Pour into bundt pan, and bake for 35-45 minutes.
